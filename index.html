Privacy Policy — OilTracker+

OilTracker+This Privacy Policy explains how I handle your data and protect your privacy.

1. Data Collection

OilTracker+ does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

  • All data you enter in the app (such as vehicle names, mileage, or oil change records) is stored locally on your device using Apple’s secure storage system (UserDefaults).
  • I do not collect analytics, tracking information, or personal identifiers of any kind.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used only within the app to provide the features you choose to use — such as tracking vehicle mileage and oil change intervals. I do not access, view, or share this data in any way.

3. Data Sharing

I do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.

4. Internet Access

The app operates completely offline and does not require an internet connection.

5. Data Security

All information you provide is stored securely on your device. If you delete the app, all related data will also be permanently deleted from your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

OilTracker+ does not contain content aimed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from users under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version available in the App Store listing or on the website.
